Why join

Established more than 70 years ago, Nelspruit tennis club is located in the heart of Mbombela city at Van Reibeeck park sports facilities and the biggest club in Mpumalanga.

We boast with a total of 9 tennis courts of which 4 is floodlit with a public park as part of the property which alos includes swimming, korfbal and squash.

NTC offers Tennis for Everyone, regardless of playing ability.

Members have access to professional coaching at any of the three coaches.

Come and join our social play or competative events or should you just want to relax with friends while having fun on court, NTC is the place in Mbombela to go to.

We are a family friendly establishment and children are most welcome.
