Tennis for Everyone

Quick start guide

Welcome to NTC!

NTC welcomes you on being a member of Nelspruit Tennis Club. We trust that you will feel at home and enjoy being part of our tennis family. We invite you to enjoy your tennis in a fun and happy atmosphere.

Situated in the heart of Clubview, within a public park area, Clubview Tennis Club (CTC) is quiet, away from traffic and yet easily accessible from the N1 highway and Old Johannesburg Road.

CTC offers Tennis for Everyone, no matter the level of playing ability. In addition, CTC offers professional tennis coaching for those wanting to improve their tennis and for the more competitive tennis player.

We welcome players of all ages and from all sections of the community. CTC maintains a balance between social and competitive play as each member has a special quality to offer at CTC.

Playing times and clubhouse hours

While members are welcome to play at all times, there are a number of activities at the club, both during the week and weekend, which take priority.

At the following times, there is organised social play for members:

  • Saturdays: From 13:00 until we run out of players.
  • Saturday: From 13:00 until late afternoon. Full members may participate.

There is no need to make an appointment or organise anything. Just arrive and participate. A member of the Management Committee will be present and assist in setting up playing teams and answer any questions you may have.

During the week, some of the courts are reserved for coaching. Other courts are available to all members on a first come, first serve basis.

The clubhouse (with bathroom facilities) will be open during official club activities and generally also when coaching is taking place.

Official Clubhouse times Mondays - Thursday 11h00 - 18h00 Satuyrdays - 11h00 - 18h00

Lights

There are 4 courts under lights. Full Members may play on these courts. Light keys are available for purchase at R50

Balls

Balls are available for purchase at the club.

New balls are made available for full members nn Saturdays and Wednesdays with organised play.

Second hand balls are available at the clubhouse for play on any other day. Please return after you are done playing. There is a hole in the kitchen door should teh kirchen be closed.

Wednesday evening social has an allocation of new balls and from time to time will also use good used tennis balls.

Visitors

We welcome visitors to the club. There is a R30 visitor’s fee.

Non-members visiting the club for more than 3 times must apply for full membership
